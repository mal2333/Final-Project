Hello Ruby is the world's most whimsical way to learn about computers, technology and programming. The story started with a book, and now Ruby continues her adventures.

Today, Ruby is excited. She has just organized her wardrobe, but she needs help in getting dressed. She has a special dress code for each day... Can you help her pick the right outfit? Remember, there are many right answers.

Suitable for kids age 5 years and up (but even adults might learn something new)!
This game will help children in developping computational thinking concepts in a fun way.
